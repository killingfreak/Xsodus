About Us

How It All Began

Xsodus founded in 2018. For too many families, the college admissions process was overwhelming, stressful, and ultimately unsuccessful. Xsodus was built on the belief that students can get admitted to their dream college and still have some fun in their college admissions journey.

What We Care About

Our mission is to empower students like you to unlock your full potential and achieve your educational dreams. We believe that everyone deserves access to quality education, regardless of their background or circumstances. Our most important work is sharing quality information with each of our students, combating fear with facts, empowering students to take ownership of this important step in their education.

In Our Community

As leading experts in college admissions, we are woven into the very fabric of the communities we serve. We are proud to partner with universities like University of Law, England, London Metropolitan University, Bennett University and so on and do our best to give back through our services.
